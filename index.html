Ireland

I've been fortunate enough to travel to Ireland on several occasions. The first two trips were with a music school I had attended, while on my last trip (my favorite) I was travelling alone. I spend 10 days in Galway before taking a bus to Donegal to visit some friends. Galway is filled with street performers and artists of varying types. I spent most my time walking around the city and listening to musicians.

A picture from one of my walks. (I cant remember exactly where I was)
Haiti

The past three years I've been to Haiti for mission work. Spending time at an orphanage and elderly home in Carries, as well as a school in Plaisance. I'm always suprized by how beautiful the country is, and especially by how beautiful the people are, despite the hardships they face on a daily basis.

Photo from a hike in Plaisance
New Mexico

Shortly after I graduated I went to New Mexico with my dad. we drove through and saw a wide variety of places. Including Carlsbad, White Sands, Tent Rocks, and Sante Fe.

On my way to White Sands (You can barely see it in the distance)
West Virginia

Towards the end of last summer I went on a road trip to West Virginia with one of my best friends since middle school. We spent a lot of time hiking and swimming in rivers. West Virginia may be my favorite place to hike.
